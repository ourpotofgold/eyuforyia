## 📖 The Chronicles

### I. Origins: The Golden Era
Before the fall, Eyuforyia was a world alive with rhythm, color, and telepathic memory. Its violet skies and golden twilights watched over four sovereign regions:
* **Glacia Peaks:** Northern wastes carved by wind and ice, home to slumbering titans.
* **Molten Core:** The deep volcanic heart of the world.
* **Whispering Woods:** The living, telepathic center of Eyuforyia.
* **Sky Sanctuary:** Floating archipelagos governed by Logic and the Sky Queties.

### II. The Invasion: "The Optimization"
The silence broke when geometric monolithic vessels descended from orbit, leaving green exhaust trails across the Azure Heights. The invaders—clinical alien scientists and emotionless droids—did not come to conquer. They came to *optimize*. To them, Eyuforyia is merely a resource node. Young Quetiemals were captured, their innate magical energies siphoned to act as biological batteries.

### III. The Mechanical Corruption
The invaders deployed terrain-conversion harpoons, injecting neon-green synthetic fluids deep into the earth. Living grass calcified into metallic wire, and soft earth hardened into cold, hexagonal plates known as **Tech-Biomes**. Windowless white spheres called Sentinoids now drift silently through these dead zones, harvesting planetary heat and forcing the Great Behemoths into lethal hibernation.

### IV. The Reclamation: Spark of Rebellion
From the ruins, a resistance was born. When his younger brother was taken to the Central Processing Hub, a Glacier Wolf named **Ice Heart** refused to accept the Optimization. Rescued by **Hotly**, a firebird warrior, they struck the first blow. Now, armed with scavenged alien technology (Pressure Valves, Hydro-Lenses) and the ancient Veridian Flute, the free tribes are uniting to shatter the Central AI.
