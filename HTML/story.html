THE LORE

CHRONICLES OF EYUFORYIA

A World Torn Open

Eyuforyia was once characterized by violet skies and golden twilights. This peace was violently disrupted when insect-shaped vessels descended, emitting a low-frequency vibration that signaled the arrival of alien invaders.

Led by clinical scientists and sleek droids, these invaders began a process of "Optimization," capturing young Quetiemals to use as biological batteries for their cold, mechanical ambitions.

The Mechanical Corruption

Massive harpoons seeped neon-green liquid that transformed organic matter into a Tech-Biome. Lush grass turned to metallic wire, and the soft earth calcified into cold hexagonal plates.

"They aren’t merely visiting. They’re rebuilding it. Making it theirs."

The invaders began harvesting planetary heat and drying the air, forcing the Great Behemoths into a lethal hibernation.

The United Resistance

Driven by the rescue of his brother, Ice Heart joined forces with Hotly the firebird. Together, they rallied the inhabitants of the Sky, Core, Sea, and Peaks.

Using the Veridian Flute to bypass security, they seek to dismantle the central AI and restore the natural stars to the sky.
