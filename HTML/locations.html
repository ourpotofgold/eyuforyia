THE WORLD

EXPLORE THE FRACTURED BIOMES

From Nature to Machine

Whispering Woods

The initial site of the invasion where the forest's "song" is harvested by Resonance Siphons to fuel kinetic shields.

Sky Sanctuary

A paradise of floating islands and crystal trees (the Cloud Forest) located high above the mechanical smog.

The Molten Core

An underground lava castle where Magma-Pangolins live; the invaders use it to siphon planetary heat.

Sunken Ruins

A deep-sea graveyard of an ancient civilization where the water has been turned into sterile "Dead Water".

Glacia Peaks

Home of the Behemoths, where the air is kept artificially dry by alien stabilizers to protect their machinery from rust.

Rotten Marsh

A sickly neon-green swamp where Light-Spires cast a harsh, unnatural glare, trapping the nocturnal inhabitants in a waking madness.

Tech-Shack City

The mechanical epicenter of the invasion and the final scar on the surface, housing the central AI processor.
