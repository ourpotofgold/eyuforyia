Heroes of the Resistance

From the molten depths to the frozen peaks, these are the warriors fighting to stop the Optimization.

Ice Heart

The protagonist driven by the rescue of his brother; uses frost magic and Static-Frost gauntlets.

Firebird (Phoenix)
Hotly

A fierce aerial warrior whose internal temperature rises with her emotions; rescues Ice Heart from the "Mirror of Frost".

Sky Quety
Volt

An energetic storm-engineer who optimizes the team's gear using salvaged alien technology.

Ruler of the Sky Sanctuary who values logic; she eventually commits her people to the war to prevent the sky from becoming a "ceiling".

Granite Badger

An Earth-Shield Tank with skin of reinforced stone; she provides heavy defense for the team.

Great Horned Owl
Nyx

A resident of the Rotten Marsh who uses shadow abilities to bypass enemy sensors.

Majestic Stag
Elder Hoof

Leader of the Whispering Woods whose antlers are made of cherry wood; he provides the Veridian Flute to bypass security.
